Dr. Sanaz Yousefi
D.D.S.

Dr. Sanaz Yousefi D.D.S.

“As a general and cosmetic dentist, I provide care to all ages. My favorite part about practicing dentistry is having the opportunity to provide a caring and comfortable experience for all of my patients. I value the relationships I build with my patients over time, and I consider it a privilege to be their dentist. I try my best to listen to their concerns, be thorough in their exam, and together come up with a plan that best works for them. I treat my patients the way I would want to be treated. “I believe smiling to be contagious. When my patients leave my operatory with a beautiful, healthy smile that reflects their pleasant, painless, and informative visit, I know I’ve contributed to making the world a happier place.”

When she’s not working, Dr. Yousefi likes to hike, take walks down the beach, bike, and hang out with family and friends. She also enjoys watching movies with her husband and traveling.

Education

  • Advanced Standing Program of International Dentists, ASPID/DDS from USC, 2012 -Top of the Dean’s List
